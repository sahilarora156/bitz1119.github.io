About Maims

Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Management Studies(MAIMS) is an ambitious initiative of Maharaja Agrasen Technical Education Society. The society is involved in various activities relating to academia, health care and has established institutions including Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Rohini, Delhi; a Medical College in Agroha(Haryana), and several Polytechnics. The society has also set up Maharaja Agrasen University at Baddi, Himachal Pradesh.
MAIMS, affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi. It was established in year 2003 to impart high quality education in management and journalism. It aims to develop itself into a premier centre of excellence by developing young and enterprising boys and girls into successful managers and journalists. MAIMS emphasizes on nurturing a value based managerial personality amongst students.
MAIMS offers Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), B.com (Hons.) ,Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Mass Communication [BA(JMC)] and BA(Hons.)Economics, BA LLB(Integrated) programmes. It has well qualified and dedicated faculty besides having latest state-of-art infrastructure. It also ventures into consultancy field and conducts Management and Executive Development Progammes on regular basis.

Genesis-A Beginning


AN EVENT THAT CONNECTS, AND INSPIRES. Genesis 18’ brings together some of the best talents of the country. Every year, around 6000 to 8000 students crowd the grounds of MAIMS, Rohini to participate in the GENESIS Annual Festival which is the largest annual cultural event in Rohini. Over 300 faculty members and 500 trustees guide us throughout this event. In two days more than 50 artists, performers and exhibitors participate enthusiastically making GENESIS a mega successful event, with wide – spread participation from Delhi University and Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University.
